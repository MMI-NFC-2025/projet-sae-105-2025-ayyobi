Jocelyn Bell Burnell (1943 – )

A Northern Irish astrophysicist, she discovered the first pulsars in 1967. The 1974 Nobel Prize was awarded to her PhD supervisor, Antony Hewish.

She later became a symbol of humility and late recognition.

ABOUT

My name is Nargis Ayyobi, an MMi student in Montbéliard. This individual S1 project explores the Matilda Effect, a phenomenon that makes women in science invisible. I chose Nicole-Reine Lepaute as a symbol of perseverance and knowledge.
